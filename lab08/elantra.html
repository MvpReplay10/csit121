My 2023 Elantra N

This my 2023 Hyundai Elantra N. Early 2023 I purchased this vehicle after I was hit in a car accident. This vehicles costed me $39,000 during that time. As my previous page mentioned today's price is down almsot $2,000 compared to what I paid. This vehicle is amazing! For the price you pay you get more than any other car can offer. This car has amazing performace and a great sounding stock exhaust. On top of everything the color I ended up choosing made this whole package even better. I love cars that stand out compared to the rest and that you often dont see. I feel that this happens to be one of them.

Why I Want A New Elantra N

On October 2023 I had a long day at work. I spent the entire day at a shooting range giving out ammo. Reason for this is I was in the Army and i wasnt able to shoot since I was still recovering from a prior accident. We finished up around 8 pm and on the way back home I lost consciousness while driving and drove into barbed wire leading into the woods. Due to the accident my vehicle was unfortunately totaled. Ever since then to now I havent made any new purchases. I was shooken up and knew I was soon leaving for deployment. Now that I'm back and out the military I really wanted to get my self this car. I really enjoyed it and it was perfect for me. I am currently searching for the best priced Elantra N and when I do I'd likely make that purchase again.
