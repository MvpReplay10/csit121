Lessons Learned

This project gave me an opportunity to reflect on my progress throughout the semester in CSIT121. I faced some initial challenges with layouts and validating HTML, but I gradually developed a solid understanding of the structure and styling techniques. Building a portfolio site put everything together into a consistent design.

Testing on multiple browsers and a mobile device revealed layout shifts that I corrected using media queries. I am still struggling with understanind how to use media querie. I believe I used it correctly but when I open the site on my phone the images and text doesn't fit the layout correctly. It made me more thoughtful about spacing, font sizes, and navigation patterns.

Overall, the course taught me the foundations of HTML and CSS, including structural elements, tables, and responsive design. My portfolio is a reflection of those labs and my progress in this semester. I learned alot in this semester and enjoyed many of the assingments giving me the ability to make the website however I want as long as it meets requirements. I still have alot of practice and skills to work on.
